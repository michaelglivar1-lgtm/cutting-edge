The Direction

A monastic discipline. Light as the only ornament.

Modern Minimalist is the discipline of subtraction. Pure white plaster, polished concrete underfoot, frameless glass that erases the line between inside and out. We build these homes with hidden hardware, monolithic walls, and a single horizontal gas flame as the only object of focus. The result is a residence that reads like a museum of light — cool, quiet, perfectly resolved.
