The Direction

Open the doors. The ocean walks in.

Miami Modern is built for the South Florida light — for the way the late-afternoon sun comes off the water and washes a room in gold. The architecture is permissive: full-height frameless sliding glass, white terrazzo floors that read seamlessly from indoor to pool deck, and a palette that holds its own against tropical color. Furniture stays low and linen-clad. Brass detailing brings the warmth back.
