Who We Are

Cutting Edge Design & Construction Inc ("we," "us," "our") is a licensed Florida general contractor (CGC060801) headquartered in West Palm Beach, Florida, serving Palm Beach County. This policy explains how we handle information collected through cuttingedgedesignfl.com.

Information We Collect

  • Automatic data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring page, timestamps) and basic analytics (Google Analytics) used to understand site traffic.
  • Cookies: small files used for analytics and basic site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, provide quotes, and deliver remodeling and construction services.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, lead-management platforms such as Thumbtack) strictly to operate our business, and with government agencies when required for permitting or compliance.

Data Retention & Security

We retain contact and project information only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights (Florida Residents & Others)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe consistent with applicable law, including the Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA).
